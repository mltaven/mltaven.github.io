About Me

  • I'm currently a M.A.S in the Department of statistics, School of Mathematics Science, CNU. My supervisor is Professor Hengjian Cui. I have a bachelor's degree in Applied Chemistry from China University of Petroleum (East China). My undergraduate thesis is mainly about the research of neural network in the optimization of asphalt foaming process.

Research Interests

My research interests include some sub-fields of Machine Learning:

  • Abnormal Detection including Out-of-Distribution (OOD) sample detection and adversarial sample detection.

  • Robust Machine Learning, the robustness of machine learning is reflected in the robustness of models, optimization algorithms, and loss functions. My current research work is mainly focused on using robust parameter estimation methods in statistics to learn more robust models, including low-dimensional and high-dimensional scenarios.

  • Sequence Prediction and Classification, I mainly focus on using imaging technology, transfer learning and ensemble learning methods for sequence prediction and classification.
